Job description
Job Summary
Our team of UX professionals supports the work of hundreds of engineers across several products, so there is always something happening. Our company is global, and our UX team includes members in India, Israel and the US.



We are an AI-forward UX organization and expect team members to leverage responsible AI to drive measurable outcomes. Candidates should be prepared to discuss and demonstrate how they have used AI tools or models in their past roles to improve productivity, quality, and innovation, including specific examples, quantified results, and the governance practices they followed.

Job Requirements
Minimum of 8 years designing enterprise product experiences
An online portfolio demonstrating advanced, wide-ranging experiences
In-depth professional knowledge of latest interaction patterns for web, user-centered design process, design principles and techniques
Experience using Figma to create low to high fidelity prototypes and mockups
Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to lead ideation sessions and present a strong rationale for design decisions
Individual contributor experience leading teams with complex across-feature/theme interaction
Ability to influence day-to-day design and research work that spans multiple themes
Ability to balance needs of concurrent projects and work effectively with cross-functional teams
Proven track record of defining and guiding features and themes to fit with an overall portfolio
Experience understanding the value of, collaborating with, and leveraging user experience research
Concrete examples of AI tools usage to improve outcomes (e.g., Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Bard, internal ML models, RPA, Figma Make, etc.)
Ability to quantify AI impact (e.g., time saved, quality improvements, improved usability, revenue growth, reduced errors, faster delivery)
Proficiency with approaches to AI usage (e.g., prompting strategies, data governance considerations, bias mitigation, evaluation/validation of outputs)
Practice following relevant safeguards and compliance practices (security, privacy, IP protection)


Preferred qualifications:



Master's degree in design, human-computer interaction (HCI), or equivalent professional experience
Experience advocating for user experience in defining and prioritizing themes across UX and engineering teams
Demonstrable experience with complex and modern web designs with the ability to create meaningful and delightful experiences
Experience maintaining and evolving design systems
Working knowledge of current web-based technologies and frameworks (e.g. CSS, Angular, React) to inform design decisions
Education
Master's degree in design, human-computer interaction (HCI), or equivalent professional experience
